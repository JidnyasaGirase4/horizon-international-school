Our Story

Two Decades of Excellence

Founded in 2005 with a single building and a bold vision, Horizon International School began with just a handful of classrooms and a deeply held belief: that every child deserves an education that nurtures both the mind and the heart.

What started as a small community of dedicated teachers and curious learners has grown into one of the region's most respected institutions — home to thousands of students, a world-class faculty and a sprawling, future-ready campus.

Through every stage of our growth, we have stayed true to our founding promise: to inspire minds and build the leaders of tomorrow through academic rigour, creativity and strong values.

Leadership

A Message from our Principal

At Horizon, we see education as far more than examinations and grades. We are committed to shaping confident, compassionate and curious individuals who are ready to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our doors are always open to parents and students who share our passion for learning. Together, we create an environment where children feel safe to question, to imagine and to grow into their fullest potential.

I warmly invite you to become part of the Horizon family and witness the difference a values-driven education can make.

— Dr. A. Sharma, Principal

Infrastructure

A Campus Built for Learning

Spread across acres of green, secure grounds, our campus is designed to inspire. From air-conditioned smart classrooms to specialised laboratories, every space supports curiosity and discovery.

Students enjoy a well-stocked library, advanced science and computer labs, a dedicated robotics lab, expansive sports facilities, an auditorium and safe, comfortable spaces to learn and play.

Campus & Facilities

A Campus Built for Learning & Growth

Spread across acres of green, our purpose-built campus blends technology, comfort and safety — creating inspiring spaces where students learn, play and thrive every single day.

Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

Modern, well-equipped spaces designed for holistic education.

Smart Classroom

Air-conditioned, technology-enabled rooms with interactive smart boards for engaging, modern learning.

Labs

Fully equipped science, computer and robotics labs that turn theory into hands-on discovery.

Library

A vast collection of books, journals and digital resources in a calm, inspiring reading environment.

Art & Activity Room

Vibrant, well-stocked studios where students explore painting, craft and creative activities to nurture imagination.

Sports Complex

Expansive grounds, courts and an indoor stadium supporting excellence across many disciplines.

Auditorium

A state-of-the-art auditorium for assemblies, performances, seminars and cultural celebrations.

Cafeteria

A hygienic, spacious cafeteria serving fresh, balanced and wholesome meals throughout the day.

Medical Centre

An on-campus infirmary with trained staff ensuring prompt care and student well-being.

Transport

A fleet of GPS-enabled buses with trained staff offering safe pick-up and drop across the city.

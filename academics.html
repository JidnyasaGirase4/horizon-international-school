Assessment

Assessment & Evaluation

We believe assessment should support learning, not just measure it. Our continuous and comprehensive evaluation tracks progress through projects, presentations, class participation and regular feedback — not exams alone.

Detailed progress reports, mentor check-ins and parent updates keep every learner on a clear path to growth, with timely support wherever it is needed.

Digital Learning

Every classroom is a smart classroom. Interactive boards, curated e-learning content and a robust learning platform make lessons immersive, visual and engaging for every kind of learner.

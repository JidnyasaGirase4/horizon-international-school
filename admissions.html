4

Who Can Apply

Eligibility & Age Criteria

Admission to Horizon International School is open to students from Grade 1 to Grade 10. Age is calculated as on 31st March of the academic year of admission.

  • Primary (Grades 1–5)
    6 to 10 years
  • Middle (Grades 6–8)
    11 to 13 years
  • Secondary (Grades 9–10)
    14 to 16 years

Admission to higher grades is subject to seat availability and a grade-appropriate assessment.

Documents Required

Please keep the following documents ready while applying.

Birth Certificate
Report Cards (2 yrs)
Transfer Certificate
Passport Photos
Address Proof
Parent ID Proof
Medical Record
Migration Certificate

Good to Know

  • Submit self-attested photocopies along with originals for verification.
  • Originals are returned immediately after verification.
  • Incomplete applications cannot be processed.
  • The admission form fee is non-refundable.
